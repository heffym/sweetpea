💕 Why Riquewihr first makes it better
Arriving straight into the fairy-tale village after your overnight flight means the first thing you wake up to in France is Belle's cobblestoned street outside your window. Three nights as a base means no packing and unpacking stress for the first three days — you come home to the same room each evening. And it means AOR La Table, the best dinner of the trip, happens on Day 3 when you're fully rested and settled, not exhausted from a week of travel.
🛌 Pace — built-in rest
Day 1 is gentle arrival only. Day 4 is a deliberate rest day with no castles. No day starts before 9 AM. No day has more than 2 major activities. The trip arc flows one direction the whole time — east to west — so you're never retracing steps or rushing to catch connections you booked on a tighter schedule.
