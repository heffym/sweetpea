Small Town Risk Mitigation

Potential Risks

  • • Limited market size: Approximately 3,625 potential customers within 30km
  • • Capacity constraints: Studio limited to 75% of 320-member capacity (~240 members)
  • • High fixed costs: $8,850/month (rent, teachers, misc)
  • • Seasonal fluctuations: Summer vacations, harvest season
  • • Cultural resistance: Conservative attitudes toward yoga
  • • Economic sensitivity: Income variations in small towns

Mitigation Strategies

  • • Diversify offerings: Add Pilates, meditation, wellness coaching
  • • Flexible pricing: Sliding scale, work-study programs
  • • Community focus: Position as wellness center, not just yoga
  • • Cost management: Optimize teacher scheduling, increase drop-in/tourism revenue
  • • Capacity management: Cap memberships at 230, offer virtual classes, prioritize off-site events (e.g., beach yoga)
