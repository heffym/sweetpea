Today's Schedule
🥐
Breakfast · 8:00 AM
Kouglopf & Cie ⭐ 4.7 · Best bakery in Alsace
Buttery, flaky croissants — reviewers came back an hour later for more. Takeaway only. Grab pastries and eat at the Belle fountain, two minutes away.
🏰
~10:30 AM · 1.5–2 hours
Château du Haut-Koenigsbourg ⭐ 4.7
A medieval fortress at 757m with views of France, Germany, and Switzerland. The isolation and drama immediately explain why this was the visual inspiration for the Beast's mountain castle. Audio guide available.
🍽️
Lunch · ~12:30 PM · at the summit
Le 757 — Castle Terrace Café
The castle's own terrace restaurant overlooking the entire Alsace wine valley. Tarte flambée, Munster cheese, Alsace wines. One of the most dramatically situated lunches of your lives.
🌹
Afternoon · ~2:30 PM
Colmar · Little Venice
30-min drive down to Colmar's canal quarter — flower-draped bridges, colour-washed half-timbered houses. Browse, find a canal terrace, share a Gewurztraminer. Leave by 5:30 PM.
🍽️
Dinner · ~7:30 PM
Au Tire Bouchon ⭐ 4.4 · Riquewihr
On the main street with the oldest wine cellar in Alsace next door. Choucroute garnie, escargot, baeckeoffe, local Riesling by the carafe. Open daily — book ahead for September.
💕 The Fairy Tale Moment
Standing on the ramparts of Haut-Koenigsbourg with France stretching below you, you immediately understand why Disney chose this for the Beast's solitary fortress. Isolated, dramatic, beautiful — both terrifying and romantic at once.
✦ Taxi Tip
Ask Hôtel Saint-Nicolas to arrange a full-day driver the evening before. A full-day car covering both Haut-Koenigsbourg and Colmar is much better value than individual rides and the driver will know all the good spots.

Today's Schedule
🍳
Breakfast · ~9:00 AM
Château de Nazelles terrace breakfast
Vineyard views, fresh pastries, château jams. The hosts are renowned for their morning hospitality. The castle opens at 10 AM and is only 30 minutes away — no rush.
🏰
~11:00 AM · 2 hours
Château d'Ussé ⭐ 4.5 — Where Perrault wrote Sleeping Beauty
Charles Perrault wrote La Belle au Bois Dormant here in 1697, inspired by this Loire château. Inside: a full Sleeping Beauty exhibition through the tower rooms — wax scenes of every key moment, from the fairies to the spindle to the prince's kiss. The Loire River gardens in September are stunning.
🍽️
Lunch · ~2:00 PM
Chinon riverside · goat cheese & red wine
15-min taxi to Chinon — a beautiful medieval village with riverside restaurants, famous Sainte-Maure goat cheese, and Chinon red wine. Pick up a bottle to open later on the Nazelles terrace.
🌿
Afternoon · back ~4:30 PM
Château de Nazelles · pool · last Loire afternoon
Last afternoon in the Loire. Nowhere to be. The château pool, the vineyard terrace, the garden. Open the Chinon. Watch the September evening light on the vines.
🍽️
Dinner · ~7:30 PM
La Pause du Temps ⭐ 4.5 · Amboise
Small, family-run, daily specials. Reviewers call it "one of the best meals in France" for quality and value. Roquefort tart, duck confit, fresh Loire fish, wines by the carafe. 5-min taxi each way.
💕 The Story Moment
Standing in the tower rooms where Perrault dreamed up Sleeping Beauty in 1697, looking out at the Loire forest below — the same forest that became the enchanted woods — is one of those moments that makes the whole trip feel inevitable. You're inside the original story.
✦ Taxi Tip
Pre-book your return taxi when booking the outward journey. Ask for pickup at ~2:30 PM to allow time at Chinon. The Nazelles hosts can arrange this entire day's transport for you — just ask at breakfast.
